ECOSat

ECOSat is a reasearch group who has competed in the Canadian Satellite Design Challenge. I learned and worked on interfacing with temperature sensors on the satellite using SPI and the satellites camera module with UART coded in C. Currently, I am working on finding the orientation of the satellite in relation to the sun using a popular sun pointing algorithm. This will be implemented in C and fixed-point arithmetic.

  • Website: UVic ECOSat

MicroProcessinG

MPG delivers formal training on embedded systems, circuit fundamentals, hardware design, soldering, and real-world programming. I wrote firmware which can scroll text to indicate simple song names and takes button presses to switch/stop songs. Also the LEDs on the boards were programmed to change accoring to pitch. More information can be found in the YouTube link below.
